Step-by-Step Guide to Setting Up Your Ledger Wallet

  1. Visit Ledger.com/start
    Begin by navigating to the official setup page. Make sure you’re on the genuine Ledger website to avoid phishing scams. Double-check the URL to ensure it reads “Ledger.com/start.”

  2. Download Ledger Live
    Ledger Live is the official companion app for managing your Ledger hardware wallet. Click on the download link for your operating system—Windows, macOS, or Linux. Mobile users can also find the app on the App Store or Google Play.

  3. Install and Launch Ledger Live
    After downloading the app, follow the installation prompts. Once installed, open Ledger Live and choose the “Get Started” option.

  4. Set Up Your Ledger Device

    • Initialize Your Device: Connect your Ledger wallet to your computer or mobile device using the included USB cable. Follow the on-screen instructions to set up a PIN code.
    • Write Down Your Recovery Phrase: Ledger will generate a 24-word recovery phrase. Write it down carefully and store it in a safe place. This phrase is your backup key in case your device is lost or damaged. Never share it online or with anyone.

  5. Verify and Secure Your Wallet
    Confirm your recovery phrase to complete the setup process. This ensures you’ve recorded it correctly.

  6. Add Accounts and Manage Your Crypto
    Once your wallet is set up, you can add cryptocurrency accounts through Ledger Live. The app supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin, Ethereum, and numerous altcoins.
